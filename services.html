What Amanda Offers

Therapy Services

Amanda offers individual therapy, couples therapy, and family therapy services. She specializes in working with diverse populations and has expertise in a range of issues including:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Stress
  • Relationship Issues
  • Family Dynamics

Fees and Insurance

Amanda accepts a range of insurance options and offers competitive fees for her services. Contact her for more information on insurance options and fees.

Interactive Elements

Amanda believes in the power of interactive elements to help her clients better understand their needs and how therapy can help. She offers quizzes and assessments on her website to help visitors better understand their mental health and wellness.

Mental Health Resources

Amanda provides a range of resources and information on mental health and wellness on her website. These resources are designed to support and empower her clients in their journey towards better mental health.

Approach to Therapy

Amanda has a person-centered approach to therapy, which means that she believes in putting the needs and goals of her clients at the forefront of every session. She works collaboratively with her clients to help them achieve their desired outcomes and improve their overall mental health and wellbeing.
