Therapy Services

My therapeutic approach is an integrative framework that is person-centered, trauma-informed, culturally inclusive, strengths-based, social justice conscious, and anti-oppressive. With a base of cognitive-behavioral therapy practices, your care plan will be developed for you as an individual to meet your or your family’s specific needs.
