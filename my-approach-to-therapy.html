My Therapeutic Approach:

My therapeutic approach is an integrative framework that is:

  • Person-centered
  • Trauma-informed
  • Culturally inclusive
  • Strengths-based
  • Social justice conscious
  • Anti-oppressive

As a therapist, I start from a person-centered and multicultural approach. I strive to provide a space where you can feel comfortable bringing your whole self to the session without fear of judgment or invalidation.

I want to recognize and provide space for processing events from the past and considering the systems of oppression that exist in our society, community, and familial history. You are the expert on yourself and your own experiences. I want to be the guide on your wellness journey, collaborating on goal creation and working toward healing and recovery together.

I consciously and with intent welcome individuals from all backgrounds, identities, and orientations. I have a strong desire to work with individuals from the LGBTQIA+, neurodivergent, and BIPOC community.

Sessions are a time and space for you to experience compassion, empathy, and unconditional positive regard. Your perception of the world is important to me and will aid in the therapeutic process of understanding yourself better.

I look forward to learning about your truth!
