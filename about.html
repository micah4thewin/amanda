About Me:

Amanda Watson, MA, LMHCA
(She/Her/Hers)
Welcome! I am a licensed child and adolescent therapist in the Pacific NW eager to join you on your journey toward a more balanced and happy life.

Working with professionals on my own parenting journey with a neurodivergent child inspired me to gain an education where I could pay it forward by helping others. Working as a high school soccer coach for 6 years I gained an understanding of the complex issues currently facing adolescents. After completing a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology at Oregon State University in 2020, I worked as an instructional assistant in the public school system where I found myself wanting to be able to provide more for the students than I could realistically give them. I earned my Master of Arts in Applied Child and Adolescent Psychology from University of Washington. Throughout my graduate education I gained experience working in community mental health with children and families as an associate affiliated counselor.

Positionality:

When approaching a working therapeutic relationship, I believe in the idea of informed consent and the client’s power to know who they are choosing to work with. I am a white, non-disabled, middle-class, gender queer, bisexual woman married to a man. I have years of personal experience with anxiety disorders and complex trauma that I have been privileged enough to access treatment of mental health care for.
